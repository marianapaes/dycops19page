Registration:

Registration site is now open 

Registration Fees:

Early full registration: 550 €
Student registration: 230 €
Additional paper registration: 120 €
Accompanying person registration: 200 €
Late full registration 640 €
Late student registation 300 €

Early registration deadline February 15, 2019

Online registration deadline April 18, 2019

On-site registration will open April 23, 2019

Lunches and coffee breaks are included during the conference program. Each participant needs to register. Each full registration (550 €) allows the submission of two papers and can have a maximum of one additional paper registration fee (120 €). Student registration covers one paper. (additional paper registration is not allowed).

Full and student registration includes coffee breaks and lunches, conference proceedings, welcome reception and dinner banquet.

Accompanying person registration includes the welcome reception and dinner banquet. The accompanying person has no access to the technical program of the conference.

One day registration does not include proceedings.
